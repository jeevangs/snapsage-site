Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

SnapSage ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how your information is handled when you use SnapSage.

Data Storage

All scan data, nutrition logs, DIY projects, wishlists, investigations, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

Image Processing

When you initiate a scan, your photos are sent to third-party AI services (OpenAI, Anthropic) for real-time analysis. These images are:

  • Transmitted securely over HTTPS
  • Processed in real-time for the purpose of generating analysis results
  • Not stored permanently on external servers by SnapSage
  • Subject to the respective privacy policies of OpenAI and Anthropic

Photos are only sent when you explicitly tap the analyze button. No images are sent automatically or in the background.

HealthKit Data

If you grant permission, SnapSage reads health data (such as weight, height, and activity levels) from Apple HealthKit to personalize nutrition insights. This data is:

  • Used only within the app for personalization
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Never sent off your device
  • Never used for advertising or marketing

Data Collection

SnapSage does not collect, store, or share personal information with third parties. Specifically:

  • We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not track you across apps or websites
  • We do not create user profiles on our servers
  • We do not sell or share data with data brokers

In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are managed entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework. We do not process, store, or have access to your payment information.

API Keys

API keys required for AI services are stored securely in the iOS Keychain with device-only encryption. They are never stored in plain text or transmitted insecurely.

Children's Privacy

SnapSage is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at snapsagesupport@gmail.com.
